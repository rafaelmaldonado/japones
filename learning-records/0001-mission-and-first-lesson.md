# 0001 — Mission set, started with hiragana vowels

**Date:** 2026-07-16

## Context
First session. Learner is a total beginner. Mission established: learning
Japanese as a personal challenge, motivated by the *feeling of steady progress*
rather than a fixed goal. Sessions are short (5–10 min).

## Decisions
- **Curriculum path:** hiragana → katakana → survival vocab → simple grammar.
  Reading-first (recognition before handwriting), per Tofugu.
- **Started with the five vowels (あいうえお)** — the true foundation, since
  every kana column builds on them. Also teaches the a-i-u-e-o ordering.
- Built shared assets: `style.css`, `quiz.js` (recall-quiz widget).
- Created `reference/kana-chart.html` with a "learned = red" progress mechanic
  to reinforce the sense of steady progress (ties to mission).

## Zone of proximal development
Next lesson: the K-column (か き く け こ). Interleave the vowels into that
quiz for spacing/retrieval practice.

## Open questions
- Confirm learner is comfortable with romaji-based mnemonics before piling on
  more columns.
