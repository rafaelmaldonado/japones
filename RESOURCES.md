# Resources

## Knowledge

- **[Tofugu — Learn Hiragana](https://www.tofugu.com/japanese/learn-hiragana/)**
  — Trust: high. Mnemonic-driven, reading-first hiragana guide. Primary source
  for the kana lessons. Free charts + online quiz.
- **[Tofugu — Learn Katakana](https://www.tofugu.com/japanese/learn-katakana/)**
  — Trust: high. Companion to the hiragana guide.
- **[Tae Kim's Guide to Japanese Grammar](https://guidetojapanese.org/learn/grammar)**
  — Trust: high. Classic free grammar reference for when we reach grammar.

## Skills / Practice

- **[Tofugu Hiragana Quiz](https://kana-quiz.tofugu.com/)** — Free recall-based
  kana drill. Good for spacing/retrieval practice between lessons.

## Wisdom / Community

- **[r/LearnJapanese](https://www.reddit.com/r/LearnJapanese/)** — Large, active,
  beginner-friendly. Has a detailed "Start Here" wiki. Good place to test skills
  and ask questions once past the absolute basics.
